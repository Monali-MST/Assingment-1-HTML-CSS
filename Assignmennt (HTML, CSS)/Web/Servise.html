Time-Attendance

The complexity of time attendance varies every company, depending on structure, hierarchy, and operational requirements.


The complexity of time-attendance varies by organisation, depending on its structure, hierarchy, and operational needs. Because of the wide range of attendance, timing, and leave restrictions, it is difficult to record and automate employee time-attendance standards. From recording entrances and exits, HR Team flexible Time-Attendance policies, shifts, and leave policies satisfy the diverse needs of various functions, departments, hierarchies, locations, and individuals.

Leave Management

When a company operates on a large or medium size, it has different attendance policies for each location.


When it comes to handling leave, each company has its own set of requirements. When a company operates on a large or medium size, it has different attendance policies for each location. For HR/Admin, accumulating multiple regulations and handling leave becomes a mammoth undertaking. HR Team leave management gives you a lot of flexibility when it comes to defining different sorts of leaves, leave accumulation criteria, leave balance regulations, and auto leave credit. Employees and reporting officers can keep track of their leave requests, approvals, and balances.

Integration with Payroll

Accuracy in the payroll system proves to be a harrowing task for HR.


HR finds accuracy in the payroll system to be a difficult endeavour. HR Team provides integration solutions with third-party systems, which saves time and money for any firm. ERP, Payroll, HRMS, Active Directory, Tally, and other programmes can all be simply integrated. Integration can be accomplished through a variety of mechanisms, including database to database, application programming interface, and offline export.
